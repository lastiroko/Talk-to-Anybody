# SpeakCoach Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-05-11

This Privacy Policy explains how SpeakCoach ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the SpeakCoach mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Information we collect

### a) Account information
When you create an account, we collect:
- Email address
- Encrypted password (we never see your plaintext password)
- Account creation timestamp

### b) Practice data
When you use the App, we collect:
- Audio recordings of your speech practice sessions
- Transcripts of those recordings generated by a third-party speech-to-text provider
- Practice progress: days completed, exercises completed, anxiety ratings, game scores
- Generated coaching feedback and scores

### c) Device & usage data
- Device type and operating system version
- App version
- Crash and error reports (anonymized)
- Notification tokens (only if you opt in to notifications)

### d) Information we do **not** collect
- We do **not** collect contacts, calendar data, photos, location, or any data outside the App.
- We do **not** sell your data to advertisers or third parties.

## 2. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide speech analysis and coaching feedback
- Track your progress across the 60-day program
- Send daily practice reminders if you opt in
- Process subscription payments via RevenueCat
- Improve the App's coaching quality (in aggregate, never tied to your identity)
- Comply with legal obligations

## 3. Third-party services

We use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policy:

| Service | Purpose | Data shared |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Supabase | Authentication, database, file storage | Email, encrypted password, progress data, audio recordings |
| Deepgram | Speech-to-text transcription | Audio recordings (deleted from Deepgram after transcription) |
| Anthropic | AI coaching generation | Anonymized transcript (no email or user ID) |
| RevenueCat | Subscription billing | Purchase receipts only |
| Apple / Google | Payment processing & push notifications | As required by their platforms |
| Sentry (if enabled) | Crash reporting | Anonymized stack traces and device info |

We do not use advertising networks, analytics that share data with advertisers, or marketing platforms.

## 4. Data storage and security

- All data is transmitted over HTTPS.
- Audio recordings and database records are stored on Supabase (EU region by default).
- Passwords are hashed with bcrypt-level security by Supabase Auth.
- Row-level security (RLS) ensures you can only read your own data.
- Audio recordings are only accessible via signed URLs that expire after one hour.

## 5. Data retention

- **Account data:** retained until you delete your account.
- **Audio recordings:** retained for 90 days, then automatically deleted unless you mark a recording as a favorite.
- **Transcripts and coaching feedback:** retained for the lifetime of your account so you can review historical sessions.
- **Anonymized aggregate data** (e.g. average WPM across all users): retained indefinitely.

## 6. Your rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your data — export from Settings → Your Data → Export
- **Delete** your account and all associated data — email `support@speakcoach.app` or use Settings → Delete Account
- **Correct** inaccurate data — edit your profile in Settings
- **Withdraw consent** for notifications and optional analytics at any time
- **Lodge a complaint** with your local data protection authority

EU users have additional rights under the GDPR.

## 7. Children's privacy

SpeakCoach is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have, contact us immediately at `support@speakcoach.app`.

## 8. International transfers

If you use SpeakCoach outside the EU, your data may be transferred to and stored in our EU-region Supabase servers. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

## 9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via an in-app notice. The "Last updated" date at the top of this policy will always reflect the most recent revision.
